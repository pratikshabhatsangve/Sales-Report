Sales Samples Report Dashboard
The Sales Samples Report Dashboard provides a detailed analysis of sales performance using interactive visualizations and key performance indicators (KPIs). The dashboard enables users to monitor revenue, customer activity, product performance, and sales trends across different countries and years.
Key KPIs
Total Sales Revenue
Total Orders
Customer Analysis
Overall Sales Performance
Dashboard Visualizations
1. Sales by Product Line
A donut chart displays the contribution of each product line to total sales, helping identify the most profitable product categories.
2. Sales by Customer
A column chart compares sales generated by different customers, highlighting top-performing customers and revenue contributors.
3. Sales by Deal Size
A pie chart shows the distribution of sales across Small, Medium, and Large deal sizes, providing insights into business transaction patterns.
4. Monthly Sales Trend
An area chart visualizes monthly sales performance, helping identify seasonal trends and peak sales periods.
5. Yearly Sales Trend
An area chart presents year-over-year sales performance, allowing users to evaluate business growth over time.
Filters and Slicers
Country Filter: Analyze sales performance for specific countries.
Year Filter: View sales data for selected years.
